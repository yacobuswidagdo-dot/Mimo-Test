Description

A creative and detail-oriented Digital Design Specialist with strong expertise in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.) and Canva to join our growing team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing visually engaging digital content, managing marketing campaigns, and driving brand awareness across various online platforms. This position is ideal for someone with a passion for design, digital trends, and data-driven marketing strategies.

Responsibilities

  1. Content Creation & Design:
    • Design high-quality visual content (social media posts, banners, infographics, ads, etc.) using Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) and Canva.
    • Ensure brand consistency across all digital assets and campaigns.
  2. Market & Trend Research:
    • Stay updated on design trends, digital marketing best practices, and competitor strategies.
    • Provide creative input and innovative ideas for campaigns.
  3. Analytics & Reporting:
    • Support performance tracking and reporting of content and campaigns.
    • Use insights to refine creative strategies and improve engagement.

Qualifications

  1. Education:
    • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Graphic Design, Digital Media, or a related field.
  2. Experience:
    • 1–3 years of experience in digital marketing, content creation, or graphic design.
    • Proven portfolio of visual work, including social media graphics, ads, or campaign visuals.
  3. Design & Creative Tools:
    • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (especially Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
    • Strong working knowledge of Canva for quick-turn content creation and branded templates.
  4. Marketing Skills:
    • Solid understanding of social media platforms, digital content trends, and online branding.
    • Familiarity with email marketing platforms (e.g., Mailchimp, HubSpot, or similar).
